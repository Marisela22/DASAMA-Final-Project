Our mission

Park St. Garage has been serving the Madison community with top-quality auto care since 1968. Founded by Charlie Ihm, the business has been in the capable hands of his son Carl and his partner Dave for the past 30 years, bringing a wealth of experience to every customer interaction. At Park St. Garage, we pride ourselves on our commitment to exceptional service and customer satisfaction.

Whether you need routine maintenance or major repairs, our team of skilled technicians is equipped to handle all of your automotive needs. We invite you to visit us today and experience the Park St. Garage difference firsthand. And if you're in the neighborhood, feel free to drop by and say hello!

Popular questions

How often should I get an oil change?

It's recommended that you change your oil every 5,000-7,500 miles, but the frequency of oil changes may vary depending on your vehicle's make, model, and usage. It's best to refer to your owner's manual or consult with one of our technicians to determine the right oil change interval for your specific vehicle.

Can I bring my own parts for installation?

Yes, you are welcome to bring your own parts for installation. However, we cannot guarantee the quality or compatibility of parts that we did not source ourselves. We recommend purchasing parts directly from our garage or other reputable sources to ensure the best possible results.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept cash, credit/debit cards, and personal checks with proper identification. We also offer financing options for qualifying customers. Contact us for more information about payment options.
